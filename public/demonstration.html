PolyUploader

Stopping the demonstration

        # The demonstration is no longer available on the web.


            
        When I set up the demonstration, I wanted to make my software accessible to people on MacOS and Linux and make it easier for them to discover the software. Unfortunately, this web version was very limited and full of upload bugs. Vercel (the hosting provider) limits file transfer to 5 MB and incurs a lot of crashes. I could not continue to provide such a service.



        To continue enjoying PolyUploader, please download the software (Windows). I'm sorry for people on operating systems other than Windows.
